Ethical Reflections and Reccomendations

Shannon Winner Christian

Reflecting on my research, I would like to conclude my opinion which leads me to say that
ethically the topic of Technology addiction and workaholism is not ethically correct in its own
ways, this is highly due to the risk factors that oppose the opportunities presented, through
research of my own I was able to see how the risk factors are critically damaging as they can
occur in short terms affecting mental health, where as the opportunities presented reward more
in long term after the risks have already occurred or will occur. I believe the choices presented
from this topic is more dependent on the ethics of the company and the person making them, as
the user has much more option to choose between the right and wrong choice whether they
know the outcomes of it are good or bad, whereas company like Apple is much more limited, as
they must follow the ethical standards to sell their product.
